The winning prototype

Autonomous delivery drone network

More
Privately-owned drones earn money by delivering packages. While making their rounds, they are able to charge themselves on decentralized charging stations (such as privates homes) though the use of induction pads. The drone pays for the charge with ether to the charging station, allowing its owner to profit. The money earned from every drone delivery goes back to its owner which can use it to pay for charging, along with drone maintenance and upgrades to their UAVs.
